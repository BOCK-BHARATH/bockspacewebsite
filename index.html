Space Roadmap

A clear sequence of milestones guiding our journey to the stars.

  1. Phase 0 — Training/Internship

    Space technology fundamentals, team setup and research foundation.

  2. Phase 1 — Satellite Design & Development

    Design and build advanced satellite systems and space vehicles.

  3. Phase 2 — Build Space Technology MVP

    Prototype development and space technology testing.

  4. Phase 3 — Virtual Space Facility

    Digital twin and space mission simulation in Omniverse.

  5. Phase 4/5 — Funding → Space Manufacturing

    Scale-up space operations and build manufacturing facilities.

  6. Phase 6 — Space Vehicle Production

    First 500 space vehicles will be manufactured (20 vehicles will be launched in the first round).
